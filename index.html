Central Asia especially sunny Uzbekistan is rightfully considered the undisputed leader in the taste of fruits, dried fruits and vegetables. Today, natural, organic and ecological products of the Uzbek lands are considered to be in demand all over the world.

  • Today, the company "LORD FRUITS" has become one of the leaders in the cultivation, preparation and export of dried fruits and nuts in Central Asia.
  • Currently, our company has its own 12 wholesale points abroad, close cooperation with tens of importers, distributors, sales agents in 8 countries around the world.

Our company is the successor of our ancestors who have grown, dried, prepared and exported since 1923 to neighboring countries. They embodied their gardens as the meaning of life, a way to support a family and get rich. It's not a secret to anyone that this served as a unique climate of Central Asia.
